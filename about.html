🌿 About Logan

Hi, I’m Logan — a local teen running a small yard care business.

I’m 13 years old and I started Logan’s Lawncare Services to help neighbors in Lutherville Timonium with honest, dependable lawn work. I focus on a small local area so I can respond quickly and keep quality high.

I only serve Lutherville Timonium, MD 21093 and nearby nearby neighborhoods. That local focus means your yard gets more attention and less travel time, and I can work around local schedules the way a bigger company can’t.

My work is manual and straightforward: clean mowing, edging, yard cleanup, hand‑pulling weeds, pressure washing, gutter cleaning, and small tree or hedge trimming. I do not apply pesticides, herbicides, fertilizers, or other chemical treatments.
