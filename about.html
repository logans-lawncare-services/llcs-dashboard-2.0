About Me

Hi, I'm Logan, the 13‑year‑old owner of Logan's Lawncare Services, a local lawn care business built to help neighbors keep their yards looking clean, healthy, and taken care of. I started this business because I enjoy working outside and wanted to offer a reliable, affordable option for people right here in my community instead of them having to call a big company.

When I'm on your property, I treat your yard like it's my own—sharp mower blades, clean edging, careful weed whacking around fences and beds, and full cleanup before I leave. I keep my prices simple with flat rates and clear quotes, and I focus on being on time, respectful, and easy to work with for every local customer I serve. Whether you need a one-time touch-up or regular mowing and maintenance, my goal with Logan's Lawncare Services is to give my neighbors a yard they can be proud of while supporting a local, youth-run business.
